## The Technology Behind the Magic

Flymoon combines:
- **FlightAware's AeroAPI** for real-time aircraft data
- **Skyfield astronomical library** for sub-arc-second celestial calculations  
- **JPL ephemeris data** for precise Sun and Moon positions
- **Angular separation algorithms** to predict alignments
- **Leaflet mapping** for intuitive visualization
- **Telegram Bot API** for instant notifications

All wrapped in a clean, modern interface that just works.
